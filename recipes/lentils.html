Lentil Recipe

Recipe Description

This is one of my favorite recipes for lentils. It is easy to cook and is very filling.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg green lentils
  • 1 whole onion
  • 4 heads of garlic
  • 5 carrots
  • 1 head of broccoli

Steps

  1. Add water to pot.
  2. Chop all vegetables as water is slowly boiling
  3. Once water is boiling, add in lentils.
  4. Stir lentils and slowly add in the vegetables
  5. Stir until lentils are fully done and then the dish is complete.
