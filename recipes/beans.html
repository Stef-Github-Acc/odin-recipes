Beans Recipe

Recipe Description

This is one of my favorite recipes for beans. Like lentils, it is easy to cook and is very filling.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg red beans
  • 1 whole onion
  • 4 heads of garlic
  • 5 carrots
  • 1 head of broccoli
  • 2-3 bell peppers
  • Tablespoon of flax and hemp seeds
  • Tablespoon of tumeric
  • Tablespoon of nutritional yeast

Steps

  1. Add water to pot.
  2. Add beans to pot
  3. Rinse beans and fill pot again
  4. Let beans sit overnight in the pot with water
  5. Rinse beans and fill pot again
  6. Chop all vegetables
  7. Heat the water to boiling
  8. Stir beans and slowly add in the vegetables
  9. Stir until beans are fully done and then the dish is complete.
  10. Sprinkle seeds and nutritional yeast on top
