Vegetable Mix Recipe

Recipe Description

This is one of my favorite recipes. It is easy to cook and is very filling, just like the mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cabbage head
  • 1 whole onion
  • 4 heads of garlic
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 head of broccoli
  • 1 beat
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 2 cups of quinoa
  • 1 can of chickpeas

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables
  2. Add vegetables to pot and stir while on high heat, proceed to add in chickpeas.
  3. Add quinoa in another pot with water, as the water boils, proceed to stir
  4. Stir until vegetables are fully done.
  5. Serve in different plates, take from each as needed
