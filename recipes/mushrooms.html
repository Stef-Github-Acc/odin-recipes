Mushrooms Recipe

Recipe Description

This is one of my favorite recipes for mushrooms. It is easy to cook and is very filling, just like beans.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of white mushrooms
  • 1 whole onion
  • 4 heads of garlic
  • 3 carrots
  • 1 head of broccoli
  • 1/2 of a beat
  • Tablespoon of flax and hemp seeds
  • Tablespoon of tumeric
  • Tablespoon of nutritional yeast

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables
  2. Add mushrooms and stir while on high heat.
  3. Stir mushrooms and slowly add in the vegetables (except for the beat)
  4. Stir until mushrooms are fully done (significantly smaller in size) and then slice the beats on top and the dish can be served.
  5. Sprinkle seeds and nutritional yeast on top
